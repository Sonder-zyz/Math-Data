设 $b_{ij} = (a_{i1} + a_{i2} + \cdots + a_{in}) - a_{ij}(i,j=1,2,\cdots,n),$ 求证：
\[
\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
  b_{11} & \cdots & b_{1n} \\
  \vdots &   & \vdots \\
  b_{n1} & \cdots & b_{nn}
\end{array}\right|
=(-1)^{n-1}(n-1)
\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
        a_{11} & \cdots & a_{1n} \\
        \vdots &   & \vdots \\
        a_{n1} & \cdots & a_{nn}
      \end{array}\right|
\]

证：
\begin{equation}
    \left( \begin{array}{ccc}
        b_{11} & \cdots & b_{1n} \\
        \vdots &   & \vdots \\
        b_{n1} & \cdots & b_{nn}
      \end{array} \right)
=\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
a_{11} & \cdots & a_{1n} \\
\vdots &   & \vdots \\
a_{n1} & \cdots & a_{nn}
\end{array}
\right)
\left(
\begin{array}{cccc}
  0 & 1 & \cdots & 1 \\
  1 & \ddots &   & \vdots \\
  \vdots &   & \ddots & 1 \\
  1 & \cdots & 1 & 0
\end{array}
\right),
\end{equation}

\begin{equation*}
  \left| \begin{array}{cccc}
  0 & 1 & \cdots & 1 \\
  1 & \ddots &   & \vdots \\
  \vdots &   & \ddots & 1 \\
  1 & \cdots & 1 & 0
  \end{array} \right|
  =(-1)^{n-1}(n-1)
\end{equation*}
对 $(1)$ 左右两边同取行列式即得要证明的等式.
